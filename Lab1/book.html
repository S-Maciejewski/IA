Description

Great book about adventures of Zbyszko z Bogdańca, young, charming polish knight who just can't get over his hate for Krzyżacy (teutonic order knights)

Follow Zbyszko's exciting adventures, including attempting to assasinate diplomats, almost having his head cut off, proposing to a little girl and being furious about not being acccepted by her father, and many, many more!
